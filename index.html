Security

Security and privacy built into the foundation

SignatureCat is designed around least-privilege access, strong encryption and EU data residency - so you can roll it out with confidence.

Keys in Secret Manager

Service-account private keys live only in Secret Manager - never in our database, logs or responses.

Encrypted at rest

OAuth tokens are encrypted with AES-256-GCM. In-memory key cache expires in ≤ 5 minutes and never touches disk.

Organization data isolation

Every operation is scoped to your Workspace and fully isolated for security.

Resistant to code injection

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How does SignatureCat update Gmail signatures?

The tool connects to your Google Workspace via API, so the data is always up to date and comes straight from your Workspace Directory.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes. Every new Workspace starts with a 7-day free trial with full access. We collect a card at sign-up but do not charge during the trial - cancel any time before it ends and you are not billed. After the trial, billing is per active user, starting at $0.80 / user / month.

Where is my data stored?

All data lives in EU regions. We don't store your end-users' personal attributes - names, phones and photos are fetched live at apply time and discarded.

Can I target only some users?

Yes. You can target specific users, entire Groups or Organizational Units. Targets are expanded to the right people at apply time.

What happens when someone changes their job title?

Because variables resolve live from the Directory, a daily reconciliation keeps signatures current automatically. Update the title once in Workspace and it propagates everywhere.

What if I need signatures in multiple languages?

You can set a separate signature language for each foreign branch - dedicated templates and assignments per branch. The tool is built for multilingual organizations.

Do you offer support with rolling the tool out in my company?

Yes. We provide professional support for implementing the tool in your company. Our experience as Google Workspace administrators makes the rollout quick and smooth. We also create custom signature templates on request - just write to contact@signature.cat.
